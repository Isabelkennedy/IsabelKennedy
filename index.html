About Isabel Kennedy

Isabel Kennedy is a creative coach and career coach based in Berlin, helping burned-out professionals make aligned career decisions through creative methodologies and body-based decision-making tools.

With over 10,000 hours of direct executive interviewing experience across global pharmaceutical and biotech sectors, Isabel brings a unique perspective to professional development. She's conducted thousands of interviews, hired teams for critical initiatives including Ebola and COVID vaccine development, and worked with executives developing radioligand therapy programs.

In a time of AI, what is happening to purpose?

Isabel explores this question through her work, helping professionals navigate career transitions while maintaining meaning and authentic connection to their work.

Now the founder of Kennedy Creative Consulting, Isabel combines traditional coaching with creative practices including ceramics workshops, meditation, and embodied decision-making frameworks.

Isabel is also a marathon runner, practicing ceramicist, and dedicated meditator with 15 days of Vipassana meditation in total silence. She brings the same discipline and curiosity from these practices into her work with clients.
